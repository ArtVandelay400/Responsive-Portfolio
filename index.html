About Me

I am a Denver native who has lived in Colorado for most of my life. I love living here and am a big fan of all the Denver sports teams. I also enjoy hiking, cooking, gardening, and gaming. I'm married and have a dog and two cats.

I am currently employed as a Sales Engineer for a software company and am hoping the skills I learn in the bootcamp will help me operate in my current position more effectively, as well as increase my options in the job market.

So far, I've found the class challenging but fun. While we cover a lot in each class, I feel infromation is delivered in manageable chunks and there's a lot of support along the way. I'm looking forward to learning Javascript and a lot of the more logic/programming driven aspects of web development.
